How we work

Clear expectations and good management make a significant difference.

Maintenance and support

Personal maintenance service with direct contact available seven days a week. Most call-outs are attended to within 24–48 hours, prioritising safety and condition.

Rent and tenancy approach

We do not increase rent during a tenancy. Our aim is long-term, stable tenancies rather than churn.

Deposits

Strong track record of fair and prompt deposit returns, supported by clear inventories and good communication.

Shared houses (HMOs)

Our HMOs sit at the top end of the local market. Rooms are large, en-suite doubles with business-grade broadband, and professional weekly cleaning included.
